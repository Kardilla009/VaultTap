What is VP?

Vault Points (VP) are not a fixed cash amount — they represent your share of VaultTap's real ad-revenue pool.

During the Accumulation Phase, you earn VP by solving daily words, completing tasks and referring friends. At the end of that phase, actual ad revenue generated by the platform is divided among all users based on their share of total VP in circulation.

Your final payout depends on real revenue generated — it is not a promised Cash amount.
